What is PrEP, and is it right for you?

PrEP (Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis) is a highly effective medication taken by HIV-negative individuals to prevent HIV infection. When taken as directed, PrEP reduces the risk of getting HIV from sex by about 99%.

Our clinic offers a welcoming, confidential environment for anyone interested in learning more about HIV prevention — no referral needed to get started.
